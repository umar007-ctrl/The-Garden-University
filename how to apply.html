How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to begin your journey at Garden University. We're here to guide you through every step of the application process.

1

Explore Our Programs

Discover the perfect program that aligns with your academic and career goals.

  • Browse our undergraduate and graduate programs
  • Attend an information session or open house
  • Connect with an admissions counselor

Take your time to research and find the program that's right for you.

2

Check Requirements

Ensure you meet all the necessary requirements for your chosen program.

  • Review academic prerequisites
  • Check application deadlines
  • Note any standardized test requirements
  • Understand English language proficiency requirements (if applicable)
3

Prepare Your Documents

Gather all required documents to complete your application.

  • Official transcripts from all institutions attended
  • Letters of recommendation (typically 2-3)
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Resume or CV (for graduate programs)
  • Test scores (if required)
4

Submit Your Application

Complete and submit your application through our online portal.

  • Create an account in our application system
  • Complete all sections of the application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Pay the application fee or request a waiver
  • Submit your application before the deadline

Application Requirements

Undergraduate Applicants

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 (varies by program)
  • Official high school transcripts
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional for most programs)
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)

Graduate Applicants

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (varies by program)
  • Official college transcripts
  • Letters of recommendation (2-3)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Resume/CV

International Applicants

  • All standard requirements plus:
  • TOEFL/IELTS scores (if applicable)
  • Credential evaluation (for non-U.S. transcripts)
  • Financial documentation
  • Copy of passport
