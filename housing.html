Housing FAQs

Answers to common questions about living on campus

What are the housing application deadlines?

The priority deadline for fall semester housing is Dec 29th, and for spring semester is November 1st. Applications received after these dates will be processed on a space-available basis. We recommend applying early as spaces fill quickly.

Can I choose my roommate?

Yes! You can request a specific roommate when completing your housing application. Both students must request each other for the assignment to be honored. If you don't have a roommate preference, we'll match you with someone based on your lifestyle preferences.

What items are prohibited in residence halls?

Prohibited items include candles, incense, hot plates, halogen lamps, pets (except fish in small tanks), and weapons of any kind. A complete list is provided in the housing agreement and resident handbook you'll receive upon assignment.

Is housing available during breaks?

Most residence halls remain open during spring breaks, though dining options may be limited. During winter break, only designated halls remain open for an additional fee. Summer housing is available through a separate application process.

What support services are available for residents?

Each residence hall has professional staff including Resident Directors and Resident Assistants who provide support and programming. We also offer academic support, counseling services, and 24/7 maintenance response. Special interest housing communities provide additional resources for specific student populations.
